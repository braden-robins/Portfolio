Visual-Design Principles:

These principles are essentially like rules or guide lines that allow our website to be visually balanced, simple, yet interesting at the same time. Things such as scale, visual hierarchy, balance, contrast, and the gestalt principle, help catch the users attention and bring out key information in a website.
